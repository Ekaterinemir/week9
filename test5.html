When is the Best Time to Visit Georgia?

4 Seasons in Georgia

You can visit Georgia the whole year round, depending on what you want to see and do during your trip. If you want to do some hiking, then the period between May and September would be the best time. All the roads to the mountains are open and the temperature is pleasant for camping.Spring brings mild temperatures and blossoming landscapes, perfect for outdoor exploration.It's also the only period in the year that you can visit Tusheti, Georgia's most remote region and Khevsureti. The landscapes there are absolutely breathtaking and you'll find some very unique and ancient settlements. Svaneti, another breathtaking mountain region of Georgia, is accessible all around the year though and you can enjoy hiking in summertime, in winter, and immerse yourself in culture and traditions, or learning about ancient legends during fall and spring. If you love skiing and you don't mind the cold and snowy weather, then you should visit Georgia between the months of December and March. Georgia is just magical during the winter! It might be cold but everything turns into a beautiful winter wonderland.Winter in Georgia is a paradise for winter sports lovers! Georgia has several ski resorts. Gudauri is a 3 hours drive from Tbilisi and probably the most popular due to its closer location to the capital Tbilisi. Svaneti has 2 ski resorts in Mestia and Tetnuldi. This is the best option for more advanced and adventurous skiers and snowboarders.Bakuriani ski resort is good for professionals and is also child-friendly. Georgia is a small country so you might think it can easily be covered in a week. But the reality is that Georgia is very diverse and has so much to offer.
